Pancakes

Description

Amazing Dutch pancakes

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Toppings
  • Syrup
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Mix Milk, Flour and Eggs together
  2. Heat up a frying pan and add a little butter
  3. Cover pan surface with batter
  4. Add toppings
  5. Turn once top of batter has dried
  6. Repeat until done
  7. Serve with Syrup and Raisins
