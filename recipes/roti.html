Roti

Description

Roti is a dish from Surinam that has become very popular in The Netherlands. It combines elements from several different kitchens around the world to make a varied and tasteful meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 4 eggs
  • 0.5kg potatoes
  • Masala spices
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps

  1. Combine flour, oil and water into a soft dough and let rest for 1 hour
  2. Boil eggs and potato for 10-15 minutes
  3. Finely chop onion, tomato and garlic
  4. fry onion in oil until translucent
  5. add tomato, garlic and masala spices
  6. add potato and eggs and let it boil for 15-20 min
  7. divide the dough in 4-6 equal pieces
  8. roll into a small pancake
  9. Bake the pancake on a hot stove
