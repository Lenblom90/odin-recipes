Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti alla norma is a vegetarian dish which tastes wonderful for something so easy to make. It utilizes the flavors of the different ingredients which work very well together.

Ingredients

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggplants
  • 1 handful of Basil
  • 6 tbsp Oil
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 4-6 Tomatoes
  • 1-2 Onions
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the onions, tomatoes, eggplant and garlic
  2. Fry the eggplant in oil until golden and let it dry
  3. Fry the onions until translucent
  4. Add tomatoes and garlic
  5. Let simmer for 15-20 min
  6. Cook spaghetti for 10-15 min in salty water
  7. Add a little spaghetti water to the sauce
  8. Add eggplant and basil
  9. Serve with grated parmesan cheese
